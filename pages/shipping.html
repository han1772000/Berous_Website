Shipping & Return Policy

Shipping Policy

SHIPPING: After you pay, normally Berous will arrange delivery the goods within 5-7 working days according to the order date. Unless the product is out of stock or partially out of stock, we will email you the order status. If the goods are not delivered within 5-7 working days, you can also leave a message to us or contact us via e-mail: info@berouspedicuresets.com

Return & Exchange Policy

Berous are very aware that returning products is a part of doing e-commerce so we implemented our own return policy. Please read carefully our return process below. If you are not satisfied with your purchase, we will gladly help you to return the product according to the guidelines below.

RETURN POLICY:

We accept returns of undamaged goods, in the original packaging, within 7 days from the date of receipt. No other fees will be charged but customer is responsible for return shipping charges.

DISCLAIMER:

Berous accepts no responsibility for color which may differ from prospectus, web sites or other promotional items.

RETURN GUIDELINES:

  • Items must be returned within 7 days from the date of receipt.
  • All items must be in the original packaging, and in good condition.

NON-RETURNABLE ITEMS:

Items that have been damaged through customer use or abused cannot in any circumstances be returned to Berous.

EXCHANGES:

Berous accepts exchanges of items purchased. Nevertheless, customer is subject to any associated shipping charges.

REFUND:

Berous will send the refund via original way that the payment made within 3-5 days when we received and checked the returns.

SOLE DISCRETION:

Berous reserves the right to deny any return at its sole discretion. Additional exclusions may apply.
